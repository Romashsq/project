SOL - Solana Fundamentals

Discover why Solana has become one of the fastest-growing blockchain ecosystems. Learn about its unique Proof of History consensus, high-speed transactions, and why developers are flocking to build on this innovative platform.

45 minutes
Video Lesson
Beginner Level

Lesson Notes & Key Takeaways

Proof of History (PoH)

Solana's unique consensus mechanism that timestamps transactions before they're processed, enabling incredible throughput of 65,000+ TPS.

Low Transaction Costs

Average transaction fee is $0.00025, making it ideal for micro-transactions and high-frequency trading.

Ecosystem Growth

Over 400 projects built on Solana including Serum DEX, Raydium, and Magic Eden NFT marketplace.

Developer Friendly

Supports Rust and C programming languages with extensive documentation and developer tools.
